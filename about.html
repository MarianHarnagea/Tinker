We transparently build high-quality minimal watches from the finest components and materials.

Tinker Watches started with a question: How can we make a watch that is clean and timeless without being too serious, thoughtfully-detailed without overt branding, honestly-priced and well-constructed, without unnecessary features or superfluous detailing?

As a team of mechanical engineers and designers, we then 3D modeled our idea and began sourcing: We scoured tanneries from the U.S. to Australia, factories in China that machine the highest-grade steel, and tested movements from Japan to Switzerland. After finding the best producers and materials, we made the buying experience straight-forward and simple: You choose the size, strap color, and finish. This is Tinker.

Water-resistant case

Our watches are water resistant up to 5 Atmospheres.

Three hands & the date

Never forget the hour, minute, second, date or day of the week.

Bilingual

You say Sunday, I say Domingo. Take your pick with our bilingual date feature.

Faceted case

Our unique shape looks like a circle from above, but is a hand-buffed icositetragon upon closer inspection.

Case, in point

Our cases are made with an extra low carbon steel often used in surgical instruments and marine applications due to its high resistance to corrosion.

It’s not glass

Our watches are protected by a sheet of sapphire crystal. Known for its scratch-resistance and remarkable hardness (nearly as hard as diamonds). Also used by a certain fruit company in some of their mini wrist computers.

I like to move it

At the heart of our watches lies a Swiss Ronda Movement. Powered by the vibration of a quartz crystal (32,786 times per second) under current to keep incredibly accurate time.

Buon Giorno

Our straps are made from high-grade vegetable-tanned Italian leather with a soft nubuck lining for extra comfort.
